Dr Puneet Gupta

GMC: 5206982

Dr. Puneet Gupta is a experienced cosmetic doctor with special interest in minimally-invasive aesthetic treatments. He is a Microcannular (MicroLipo) and VASER Lipo Body Contouring specialist and is currently one of the very few physicians in the UK and Europe practicing Microcannular Tumescent Liposuction. He is a Member of the British Association of Aesthetic Medicine and of the American Academy of Aesthetic Medicine.


“If you want great legs, there is no greater doctor to get them from” * Sophie Bloomfield, Beauty Director, Harper’s Bazaar


Dr Gupta prides himself in his innovative approach to body sculpting. He is probably the only practicing doctor in the UK and Europe who has had extensive training in Microcannular Liposuction using tumescent anaesthesia in the USA by the inventor of tumescent MicroLipo technique – Dr Jeffrey Klein.


He has performed around 2,000 MicroLipo procedures for The Private Clinic to date, also using his specialist skills and methodology to treat patients suffering from Lipoedema.


“The benefits of Dr Gupta’s insistence on working from the knee to groin are evident to me now. My leg shape is a lot straighter and, more importantly, it looks entirely natural. Since deciding to go ahead with the operation, I have spoken more openly about the affliction I found so awkward for so long.”* Deni Kirkova, FeMail Writer, Mail Online

“The whole art of liposuction is not about how much fat to take out but how much to leave behind so that the treated areas look completely natural!”
