ABOUT THE PROJECT

Founded by in 1992, by Attorney J. Todd Tenge, the Tenge Law Firm is a boutique injury litigation firm specializing in serious personal injury and insurance cases. The law firm has its own website already but it has certain issues on the organization of content and ineffective lead generation.

With the purpose to increase the website's potential, the Project Manager (PM) approached me to redesign 2 main pages: Homepage and the Practice Area page. The project was completed in less than 5 days with 2 completed wireframed pages.

Website Wireframes

The PM sent me the website to review first before our online discussion. He also sent a Design Tweaks documents that included his notes on the issues. After the meeting, I decided to have a thorough UX review on the current website, particularly the Homepage and the Practice Area page.

Below are the points that I have come up with the UX issues.

2 - Research the Competitors

Daniel R. Rosen, The Sawaya Law Firm, Levine Law are three direct competitors of the Tenge Law Firm in Colorado.

On reviewing their websites, I didn't focus to study their layout design, interface design, and the user flow. Instead, I focused to look at their Information Architecture to answer certain IA questions:

• What kind of information do they present on the Homepage?

• What kind of information do they present on the Practice Area page?

• What types of content do they have on their website?

• What types of content do they focus and present frequently?

• What types of content is important to the customers?

• How is the content is presented together?

In Summary

THE NOT-SO-GOODs

• Sprint design is applied, therefore, in less than 5 days, I was able to design complete 2-page wireframes.

• Instead of focusing on the interface design, I switched it on the Information Architecture to analyze and put on the most important information that the customers want and need.

• By using real images into the wireframes, it helps to increase the user experience as it looks like a real website.

• I received very positive feedback from the final deliverables.
