ABOUT THE PROJECT

Tribal Blast is a shooting mobile game application that will allow multiple players to compete with each other.  The app has a beta version ready in September 2019.  The customer approached me for further help on the game testing.  So I organized a Game Testing Day with 13 players to review the app's compatibility and performance.

I have to say that we had soooooo much fun on this Game Testing Day. We had an active and exciting game playing in the city's zoo.

In the beginning, I expected that the players would go through most of the Test Cases successfully. However, there were a number of factors that happened including bugs, crashings, technical limitations, the game ideas, the UX gaps, etc. All the things made the Game Testing Day outcomes become so insightful for me and the app owners.


Currently, Tribal Blast has been removed from the Google App Store 😢.

But this is a very special project that I have worked on which I have learned and collected valuable UX lessons.

In any digital product development, UX Design doesn't stand-alone or even the only success factor.

I believe it is a tight collaboration between departments and all related factors. In 2021, I want to

learn more about the post-stage of UX Design which focuses more on user data reports.

Particularly, UX Analytics and UX Writing are areas that I want to enhance my knowledge in this year.  

From 2013 until now, I have been working remotely with individual customers, startup teams, and outsourced agencies. I really enjoy remote work and strongly believe that remote working will increase rapidly in the next coming years. My most important career goal this year is to get a full-time remote job in a product company (not an outsource) for these reasons:


1. Financial Stability: the pandemic hit hard on all countries and I believe when I have a stable income, I could maintain a good living, therefore I could maintain a good career.


2. Sharing and Learning from others: In a product company, I will have more chances to collaborate with other UX Designers, UI Designers, Developers, Content Creators, Customer Service, etc.

I am looking to work in a friendly remote company that each team member would support each other, work, and grow together with the company.


3. Evaluating my UX work with further Data Analytics: From my UX Process, I mainly work on the research, ideation, and prototyping. I always have a thirst to evaluate my UX work with further real user reports. Therefore, working in a product company, I can advance my UX knowledge with other sophisticated areas.
