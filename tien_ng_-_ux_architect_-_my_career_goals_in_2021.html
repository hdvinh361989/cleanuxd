My Career Goals in 2021

3️⃣ Get a full-time remote UX position in a product company. 🚀🚀🚀

From 2013 until now, I have been working remotely with individual customers, startup teams, and outsourced agencies. I really enjoy remote work and strongly believe that remote working will increase rapidly in the next coming years. My most important career goal this year is to get a full-time remote job in a product company (not an outsource) for these reasons:


1. Financial Stability: the pandemic hit hard on all countries and I believe when I have a stable income, I could maintain a good living, therefore I could maintain a good career.


2. Sharing and Learning from others: In a product company, I will have more chances to collaborate with other UX Designers, UI Designers, Developers, Content Creators, Customer Service, etc.

I am looking to work in a friendly remote company that each team member would support each other, work, and grow together with the company.


3. Evaluating my UX work with further Data Analytics: From my UX Process, I mainly work on the research, ideation, and prototyping. I always have a thirst to evaluate my UX work with further real user reports. Therefore, working in a product company, I can advance my UX knowledge with other sophisticated areas.


If you are looking for a UX Designer for your team/company and think I am a good fit, please don't hesitate to contact me. I always feel grateful for each opportunity and am seeking the right one for my career advancement.
