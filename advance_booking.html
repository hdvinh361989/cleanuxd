Benefits of advance booking

Validating Product Ideas Through Lean User Research

Answering the question "Do people want my product?"  is key in making you more aware of the market needs and your customers' current pain points. Consequently, before getting started on the product development, we need to evaluate the product's potential to increase its competitive advantages. For that, once you complete the booking, you will get instant help on your product idea validation. This is the most important and primary benefit when you book me in advance.

FAQ: Booking & Payment

How does the booking process work?

How much is the deposit?

1.     After you fill out the UX Advance Booking Form, you will receive my email to confirm your booking request.

2.     You will receive a payment method guide via email.

Please proceed to send the deposit to verify your booking.

3.     Please email your payment receipt after you have made the transfer

4.     When I receive the deposit, I will send you a Booking Confirmation with a Project Starting Guide.

For all project booking, I request a fix deposit of $500 usd.

What are the payment methods?

I accept payments via Bank Transfer, TransferWise, and Payoneer.

What if I cancel my booking?

For the project's success, I only focus one project at a time.

Therefore, after you made the booking successfully, I will lock my calendar and don't accept projects within your reserved periods.

Thus, I encourage that you should cancel your booking 30 days before the booked Start Date. Booking Deposit will be fully refunded.    👍

On the other hand, late cancellation will influence our work planning, my business's profit reduction and prevent other clients to book for their projects. As a result, for late cancellation under 30 days, it will be charged $200 usd for the loss cost.

Product Requirement Clarification Support

Most of my clients come to me with their great product ideas but they still lack their product details. But writing a Product Requirements Document (PRD) can be time-consuming and ineffective without certain user experience techniques.

For this reason, I will take part in producing the PRD to support the product requirement clarification which will be also an initial reference for all the stakeholders.

Successful Project Management with High-Quality Prototype Assurance

The sooner you start to get serious into the project, the better you have time to work on the development preparation. 

Advance booking helps both sides to have effective project planning. You, as a client, can reserve my availability and focus on your product in order to receive high-quality deliverables on a committed timeline.
