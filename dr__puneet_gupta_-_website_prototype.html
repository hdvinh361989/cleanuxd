ABOUT THE PROJECT

Dr. Puneet Gupta is one of the UK’s leading aesthetic physicians. He performs a wide range of non-surgical cosmetic procedures and specializes in Micro-lipo. Dr. Puneet already had his website that could potentially drive from 10 to 12 leads per month, but it still a very low number to convert into real customers. 

Therefore, this project is about to renovate his current website to enhance his reputation and image over the online platform and to help him to gain more target customers.

After the first stage, I moved to the second step to analyze the customers. 

First off, I started with the User Scenarios.
