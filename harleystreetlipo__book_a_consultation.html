Some people think it is fat, but Lipoedema or Lipedema is more than just the fatty tissues that accumulate in the buttocks, legs and thighs. Microcannular Liposuction is a technique that uses tumescent anaesthesia instead of general anaesthesia, it is considered “the safest form of liposuction”.
