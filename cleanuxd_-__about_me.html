UX Lookbook

UX is not only about the design. My work is not just about the look.

It is also the feels, the actions, interactions and acceptance from the user’s perspectives.

Life is a set of purposeful coincidences. I could only realize it when I look back on how I have become a UX Designer. In 2001, when I was in grade 6th and was just a middle-school student. Every day, after school,  I always spent my afternoon installing and checking utility software. I loved to see how I could use the software to get my study easier or simply to examine how they were designed to operate. That was a great passion back in those old days.


The curiosity grows gradually and now I have been working as a UX Designer for more than 6 years.

Today, my goal is that I can join in challenging projects that I can give all my best and strengthen my skills to develop more useful apps for the people.

Story behind my UX Career

In 2009, when I took StrengthsFinder test,  my top strength was Empathy.

Although I have taken the test long before I started my UX Career, but I always believe that this is my trait that I can work well in UXD.  Here is why Empathy is defined as The UX Pro’s Secret Weapon
