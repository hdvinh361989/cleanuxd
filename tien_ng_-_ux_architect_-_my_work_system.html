Tools I use in 2021

As designers, I do believe each will have a different set of tools. My tools are also changed and evolved over the years.

Here are my top 05 favorite tools for work:

• Setapp - a cross-platform suite of Mac apps

• Coda - a Notion-competitor but stronger with data functionalities

• Krisp - a noise-cancelling app for remote meetings

• Axure - creates hi-fi interactive prototypes

• Obsidian - writing and connecting ideas

My Work Management System

While the functionalities between Notion and Coda are quite similar, I'd prefer Coda more because each project will have a separate doc.

Now every time I work on a new project, I create a Coda doc and share with my clients to review and collaborate on the design and development.
